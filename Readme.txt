Medical A to Z is a medical dictionary android app created to aid laboratory researchers and medical students of laboratory sciences in quickly finding definitions of prefixes, suffixes, and abbreviations used in laboratory science fields.

Using Medical A to Z, it is much easier to find prefixes and suffixes used in medical laboratory fields. Moreover, each prefix and suffix has a definition, several examples and their definitions, and read-aloud buttons to read each word using the TTS engine of your phone! This provides researchers and laboratory science students to quickly and efficiently find information they need and quickly get back to focus on their work.

Above all of that, Medical A to Z includes a full list of abbreviations commonly used in laboratory science fields and their meanings, which also has a search feature to quickly provide you with the information you need in one go.

With a clean and minimal UI optimized to keep your focus intact, Medical A to Z is your very companion for your journey in learning and using laboratory sciences!
